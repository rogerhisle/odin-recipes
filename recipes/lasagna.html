Lasagna

This lasagna is very noodley and cheesy

All you gotta do is put the ingredients together properly and then you made lasagna

Ingredients

  • A buncha cheese
  • Some lasagne noodles
  • Whatever protein you want, I ain't judgin'
  • Some time
  • A way to cook that thing

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Do not unboil the noodles
  3. Put some of the cheese down on the noodles
  4. Slather that protein down
  5. Put some more of that cheese
  6. Repeat those steps until the end of time
  7. Stick that lasagna in the oven if you get bored of repeating the steps
